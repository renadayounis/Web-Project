About MGANETO

MGANETO is a clothing brand inspired by the spirit of football. Created by two 20-year-old students: Michael and Bishoy, both studying Computer Science.

We started with a simple idea: to create football-inspired designs that you can wear in your everyday life. Not match-day jerseys, not overly sporty outfits — just clean, comfortable clothing with football DNA… without looking “too sport.”

Our goal is simple: to let anyone who loves their team wear something that represents them — whether they’re going to university, hanging out with friends, heading out, or even going to training.

Every collection we drop reflects our journey: learning, experimenting, and growing the brand day by day.

MGANETO is for people who love football… and also love good style.
